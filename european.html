10 Days Exploring 5 Iconic European Cities

Embark on a journey through the heart of Europe. Our European Grand Tour takes you to 5 of the most iconic cities: Paris, Rome, Amsterdam, Barcelona, and London. Immerse yourself in culture, history, and architecture with guided tours, local experiences, and plenty of time to explore at your leisure.

Package Includes:

  • 10 days of travel through 5 European cities
  • Luxury accommodations in central locations
  • Guided city tours and visits to famous landmarks
  • All transportation between cities
  • Meals included (breakfast and some dinners)
  • Airport transfers and local transportation

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris

Arrive in Paris and check into your hotel. Spend the afternoon exploring the Eiffel Tower and enjoy a Seine River cruise before a welcome dinner.

Day 2: Paris Sightseeing

Take a guided tour of the Louvre Museum, visit Notre-Dame Cathedral, and explore Montmartre before enjoying dinner at a traditional Parisian bistro.

Day 3: Travel to Rome

Board a high-speed train to Rome. Upon arrival, visit the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and enjoy a stroll through the charming streets of Rome’s historic center.

Day 4: Rome & Vatican City

Tour Vatican City, including the Sistine Chapel and St. Peter’s Basilica. Spend the afternoon exploring Rome’s stunning piazzas and fountains.

Day 5: Travel to Amsterdam

Fly to Amsterdam and check into your hotel. Take a canal tour and visit the Van Gogh Museum.

Day 6: Amsterdam Exploration

Explore the Anne Frank House and Rijksmuseum, followed by a leisurely bike ride around the city's historic canals.

Day 7: Travel to Barcelona

Fly to Barcelona and enjoy a guided tour of La Sagrada Família and the Gothic Quarter. Spend the evening at the beach or exploring the lively La Rambla.

Day 8: Barcelona Adventures

Take a full-day tour of Gaudí’s architectural masterpieces, including Park Güell and Casa Batlló, followed by a traditional Spanish dinner.

Day 9: Travel to London

Fly to London and explore famous landmarks like Big Ben, the Tower of London, and Buckingham Palace.

Day 10: London and Departure

Take a morning tour of Westminster Abbey and the Houses of Parliament. Afterward, enjoy free time for last-minute shopping before heading to the airport for your flight home.
