5 Days Exploring the Swiss Alps

Join us for an unforgettable 5-day adventure in the Swiss Alps. Whether you're an experienced mountaineer or a first-time hiker, our Alpine adventure will take you through scenic trails, pristine lakes, and majestic snow-capped peaks. Enjoy guided tours, luxury accommodations, and thrilling activities.

Package Includes:

  • 5 days of hiking, trekking, and sightseeing
  • Stay at luxury Alpine resorts and chalets
  • All meals included (breakfast, lunch, and dinner)
  • Professional mountain guides and gear rental
  • Visit to iconic spots like Zermatt, Jungfraujoch, and Lake Geneva
  • Transfers from and to the airport

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Zermatt

Arrive in Zermatt and check into your luxurious Alpine resort. Explore the charming town and enjoy a welcome dinner with stunning views of the Matterhorn.

Day 2: Trek to Gorner Glacier

Hike to the Gorner Glacier, where you'll experience the vast ice fields and spectacular views of surrounding peaks. Return to Zermatt for a relaxing evening.

Day 3: Ride the Gornergrat Railway

Board the Gornergrat Railway for a scenic ride to the summit. Enjoy panoramic views of the Alps before hiking down to a secluded valley.

Day 4: Visit Jungfraujoch - The Top of Europe

Take a day trip to Jungfraujoch, known as the "Top of Europe." Visit the highest railway station in Europe, and enjoy snow activities and breathtaking mountain vistas.

Day 5: Departure

After a final breakfast in the mountains, transfer to the airport for your return journey home.
